About

I'm a third-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Vermont, with a deep interest in design, fabrication, and making things that are both functional and well-crafted.

My hands-on experience spans 3D printing, laser cutting, CNC milling, and CAD modeling in SolidWorks, Fusion 360, and AutoCAD. I currently work as a FabLab Technician at UVM, helping fellow students bring their ideas to life.

Outside of school, I'm a co-founder of a CNC club, a woodworker, and someone who genuinely loves the craft of building — whether it's an electric guitar cut from wood or a kit card shaped like a DeLorean.
